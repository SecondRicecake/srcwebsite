About SRC

Security Research Club (SRC, pronounced 'source') is an extracurricular group designed primarily for LIFS students to expand upon the knowledge and skills they gain in class, but is also open to anyone looking to grow their technical skillset. SRC provides the opportunities and resources that students need to be proactive in their educations and gain real, hands-on experience in their fields. As a supplement to the LIFS curriculum, SRC aims to produce competent graduates who are thoroughly prepared and ultimately qualified for the industry of their choice.
